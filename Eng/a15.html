Chapter 4: The Struggle for Love

“ M: Good afternoon! Felecita?”
“ F: Oh, it’s you, Manuel. I thought you wouldn’t come.”
“ M: Please, I cannot hide my happiness, so I am a bit nervous and unsure if I acted appropriately.”
“ F: But you need not be shy around me... we are both creations of God and have rights, and I am no different from you.”
“ M: I apologize.”
“ F: Let it be, and by the way, do you have a girlfriend, Manuel?”
“ M: Not yet, for I still have duties I must fulfill.”
“ F: Your future spouse will be lucky. I know that the city won’t change much without people like you.”
“ M: Thank you, I feel the same about you; your future spouse will be very fortunate.”
“ F: If I only had the freedom to choose a spouse, I would certainly choose you.”
“ F: Someone like you who cares for the independence and progress of the nation.”
“ M: Why, Felecita? Is there a problem you are facing?”
