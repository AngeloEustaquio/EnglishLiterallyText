Emilio eventually arrested Juan and Andres and tortured them... He threatened to execute them if they didn’t reveal where Manuel and Felecita had gone, but Juan and Andres were ready to die for Manuel.
As Manuel and Felecita reached the Sierra Madre, Manuel still felt deep sorrow over the loss of his parents... Felecita comforted him to ease his grief; every night she hugged Manuel and showed him love like a spouse.
Soon, Manuel and Felecita were married in the Sierra Madre mountains... They made love as if there were no tomorrow, though Manuel’s mind still wrestled with what he was fighting for.
With support from the people of Sierra Madre and his wife Felecita, Manuel prepared to return to Intramuros.
After a year, Manuel was ready to fight alongside his wife.
Upon returning to Intramuros, he learned that Emilio had arrested Juan and Andres, enraging him... Manuel and Felecita went to the chapel to hide while the fight was still ongoing.
At night, Manuel went to Gustavo’s house and asked where Juan and Andres had been abused... Gustavo was terrified as Manuel pointed a bolo at him, ready to cut off his hand.
