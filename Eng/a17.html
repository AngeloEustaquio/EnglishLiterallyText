“ M: I will court you. I will fight for you against your father and Gustavo.”
“ F: I will also fight for our love.”
While Manuel and Felecita were talking, they did not notice that Gustavo was watching them, which made him even angrier.
Gustavo went to Emilio and told him everything. Emilio immediately made a plan and ordered Hernando to scare Manuel to make him stop.
At dusk, Hernando went straight to Manuel’s house and held a machete to his neck. He threatened that if Manuel did not stop interfering in the government and courting Miss Felecita, he would be killed without a trial.
Because of this event, Manuel’s desire to help the people burned even more… not only to provide their needs but also to overthrow the governors who pocketed the town’s money, which was the reason why poor people could not go to school.
Two days later, Manuel went to Juan and Andres’ house. He informed them of what happened to him, and also told them that he had confessed his feelings to Miss Felecita. He also mentioned that Emilio’s son, Gustavo, was the one she was supposed to marry.
