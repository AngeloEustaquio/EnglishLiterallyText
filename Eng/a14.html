“ A: You know, Manuel, you only realize you love a young lady when you are happy with her and time seems to slow down.”
“ J: It seems you are smitten by the daughter of Senior Fernando.”
“ M: I do not know what is happening, but it seems that young lady has captured the surge of my feelings.”
“ A: You know, Manuel, Juan and I can help you win the heart of that young lady. If you wish, why don’t we serenade Miss Felecita?”
“ M: Thank you, Andres and Juan, but maybe it’s better if I take care of this myself. Besides, the progress of our nation should come first. We need to change what has long been the norm.”
“ J: That’s right, friend. We need to give the poor the right to study and provide them the funds they deserve.”
“ M: Perfect, friend. Let’s do it, we’ll get there too.”
While in class, Manuel could not hide his joy because he would soon meet Miss Felecita, which made him nervous. It seemed he wished time would speed up.
After class, he immediately went to the garden, where he saw Miss Felecita smiling while glancing at the beautiful flowers.
Manuel’s face could not hide his delight as he gazed at Felecita’s eyes, which seemed like shining pearls.
