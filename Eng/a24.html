In the Shadow of Greed
Kiko
Beside the gold and hidden wealth, Emilio, the governor, secretly rules the town, instilling fear.
Every coin meant for teachers and schools ends up in his pocket, while the town suffers and longs for justice.
Even children and beggars have rights, yet under his dark hand, there is no justice.
The land, water, and food are withheld; under his power, people are oppressed and hungry.
But the light will come, and those responsible will answer; the poor will rise, seeking revenge with poetry and arms.
Emilio, for your wickedness, you will not escape. The people of Intramuros will finally bring you to justice!
The guests’ eyes viewed Emilio as if he were a pig because of the poem.
Enraged, Emilio immediately ordered the guards to arrest Kiko for lies and defamation.
Before the guards could seize Kiko, Andres rushed in to help… Here Manuel revealed his true identity and told everyone about the evils of Emilio and his son.
