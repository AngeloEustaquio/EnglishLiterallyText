It did not take long before the Deguzman family heard Manuel singing with his friends. Felecita glanced at Manuel’s eyes as he sang, as if giving her a new sense of hope...
But before Manuel could finish his song, Gustavo immediately poured water on them, which angered Felecita.
Gustavo revealed the relationship between the young lady and Manuel, which greatly angered Don Fernando....
Don Fernando quickly drove Manuel out... and allowed Gustavo to enter their home.
Meanwhile, while Gustavo and Don Fernando discussed the upcoming wedding, Manuel quietly took Felecita to Juan and Andres’ house to hide.
When Gustavo learned of this, he immediately went to his father... Emilio arrested Manuel’s parents and ordered Hernando to kill them...
Upon learning that his father and mother were killed, Manuel was filled with grief and deep sorrow... He knew that Emilio and Gustavo would not stop until he was dead. So Manuel sought help from Juan and Andres to leave Intramuros and go to a distant town.
Juan and Andres asked their friend, the principal, for help so that Manuel and Felecita could reach the Sierra Madre to lay low and escape the chaos for a while.
