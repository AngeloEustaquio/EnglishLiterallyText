“ M: I am Manuel Dela Cruz from Bagumbayan, studying Castilian.”
“ F: So you enjoy writing.”
“ M: Yes, I have loved it since I was a child.”
“ F: By the way, maybe you’d like to join me in the garden after school.”
“ M: Let me guess, flowers are your favorite?”
“ F: Correct.”
“ M: What is your favorite flower?”
“ F: Hibiscus is my favorite. And yours?”
“ M: Jasmine, mine is.”
“ F: Alright, let’s meet in the garden around 2:30 PM, and don’t be late. You don’t know how angry I can get.”
“ M: Okay, Miss… I mean, the lovely Felecita.”
“ F: Goodbye, Manuel.”
Manuel did not know why he felt such joy after speaking with Felecita. It seemed he had fallen for her, so he immediately spoke to his two friends to share his feelings.
“ M: Juan and Andres, how do you know when you truly love a young lady?”
