“ F: I know you know Gustavo.”
“ M: Yes, is there a problem?”
“ F: Did you know he is the son of Governor Emilio?”
“ M: What?”
“ F: And he is also the one I am supposed to marry, but I do not like him. I dislike his character; he only cares for his own advancement.”
“ M: Then why would you marry him?”
“ F: Because my father wants it. In return, our family business will grow, and Governor Emilio will protect our business.”
“ F: You know, Manuel, I feel suffocated by Gustavo. I do not love him, and I do not want to marry him.”
“ M: I will court you!”
“ F: What, Manuel?”
“ M: I do not know why, but whenever I am with you, my heart is happy. From the first time I saw you, my feelings changed. Now I know I love you and I am ready to do everything for you.”
“ F: Manuel, you know you will clash with my father over this.”
“ M: I am ready to fight for you, Felecita, even if it costs my life.”
“ F: I love you too, Manuel, but how can we fight for our love if I am set to marry Gustavo? And I am sure my father will never agree.”
