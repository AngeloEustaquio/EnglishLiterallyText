Homeland
Juan Bonifacio
Philippines, island of beauty and ocean’s vigor,
Why are the poor denied freedom?
The poor should also thrive,
Yet they suffer while the wealthy prosper.
“ W: A wonderful poetic work by Juan Bonifacio. Now let us hear the allegory of the one and only Andres Luna.”
Spark of the Nation
Andres Luna
Education is needed for the nation to progress,
Why not allow the youth to study?
Those who dominate
Are the ones who oppress.
Indios who need help
Are deprived of it,
Laws are unfair,
But must be challenged.
The nation’s money vanishes,
Governors appear only when there is wealth.
Is there no hope left for the nation?
Different kinds of beasts rule,
And they must be replaced.
