But Juan and Andres were no longer surprised… because they had seen Emilio and Gustavo talking behind the stage. They advised Manuel to stay with them to remain safe from any future dangers caused by his fight against the government.
Juan and Andres knew that Emilio and Gustavo would not stop until they got what they wanted.
Juan even joked that they should serenade Miss Felecita in her yard. This way, they would show that Manuel was not afraid of Gustavo.
And Andres agreed… because this would also show Manuel’s burning heart for Miss Felecita.
Manuel began preparing to sing for Felecita, while Juan and Andres tuned the guitar and bandurria for the upcoming courtship.
Before dusk, they went to Miss Felecita’s yard, setting up the melody and music they would play.
