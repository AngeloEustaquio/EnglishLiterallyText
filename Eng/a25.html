Indio

  • English

Here are also the Indios who were deprived of freedom and wished to take revenge on Emilio.
In his extreme anger, Emilio grabbed the revolver of an Indio and fired at Manuel.
Manuel was hit in the arm, which sparked the shooting inside the casa grande.
Because of the gunfire, Juan’s group entered and shot all of Emilio’s men… Juan also killed Hernando, who needed to pay for his wrongdoings.
As Juan entered, he saw Emilio and Gustavo trying to escape. Emilio begged to let them go in exchange for a large sum of money.
However, Juan did not agree and shot Emilio and Gustavo to pay for their crimes.
Meanwhile, Juan and Andres rushed Manuel to the nearest pharmacy.
Felecita was summoned to the pharmacy to care for Manuel… Juan, Andres, and others went to Plaza Mayor to announce that the suffering of Filipinos deprived of the right to education had ended… Emilio’s rule was over.
The poor went to Emilio’s house to take the items he had bought using public funds, which then provided them relief.
